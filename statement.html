About me



I am a digital artist focused on showing in my works that it is not necessary to know how to draw to show a good idea or make art. What is necessary is to plan and analyze the different elements that we can use in our favor to transmit our message. As for example images without copyright, html codes and basic concepts of composition. Previous knowledge about the effects and tools at my disposal in programs like Photoshop play an important role in my art, because it helps me take it to the next level. When you combine all these elements I can break the barriers of what we call traditional.

Omar Antonio 2020
